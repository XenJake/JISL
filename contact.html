Admission Process

At JISL, we offer admission from Pre-K to University. Our school provides an exceptional education that helps develop students into leaders for the future. Below, you'll find the steps for applying at each level.

**Application Process**: Fill out the form below, and a representative will get in touch with you. Our admission team will provide all the necessary steps to help guide you through the application process.

Levels of Admission

  • Pre-K: Fun and interactive learning environment for our youngest students. Focus on social skills, basic numeracy, and language development.
  • Elementary (Grades 1-6): Strong foundation in reading, writing, mathematics, and science.
  • Junior High (Grades 7-9): More specialized subjects with a focus on critical thinking, leadership, and preparation for high school.
  • Senior High (Grades 10-12): Advanced curriculum with opportunities for electives, AP courses, and career exploration.
  • University: Undergraduate programs with a focus on holistic education, practical experience, and preparation for the global workforce.
