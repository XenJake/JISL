Our Programs

Science

Our Science program covers a wide range of topics, from Biology to Chemistry, Physics, and Earth Science. Students gain hands-on experience with experiments, fostering curiosity and critical thinking.

Mathematics

JISL's Math program provides a solid foundation in mathematical concepts, focusing on Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, and Calculus. Our goal is to help students develop problem-solving and analytical skills.

Health

The Health program at JISL emphasizes physical well-being, nutrition, mental health, and wellness practices. Students will learn how to make informed choices about their health and well-being.

Bible Studies

Our Bible Studies program encourages students to explore biblical teachings, ethics, and the history of Christianity. We aim to instill moral values and critical thinking about faith and spirituality.

History

JISL's History program covers global and local history, from ancient civilizations to modern-day world events. Students will analyze historical documents and develop a deep understanding of human progress and challenges.

English

Our English program focuses on vocabulary, grammar, and literature. Students will enhance their reading, writing, and communication skills while exploring various genres of literature, from classic novels to modern poetry.
